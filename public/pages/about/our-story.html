Our Story


Cousins John and Stockton established Don Juan Stockín's in the fall semester of 2019, with a vision to feed the poor college students of Liberty Square.

It all started with a simple desire to have good company over at their apartment and enjoy great food. Since then, it has grown to be so much more.

Taco Tuesday has become an opportunity for them and for those that come to pause during the busy school week and forget life's troubles, even if just momentarily. The weekly event has become a local favorite for many residents at Liberty Square in Provo, Utah.

Don Juan Stocktín's is Utah's leading non-profit taco joint, where each visitor splits the cost of the food and beverages prepared, which normally comes out to less than $3.00 a person, leaving plenty of food for everyone.

"Come for the food, stay for the amistades."
